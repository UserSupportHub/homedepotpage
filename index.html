How to Use Your Home Depot Credit Card

Activate, register, and manage your Home Depot Credit Card at HomeDepot.com/MyCard.

Go to HomeDepot.com/MyCard

1. Activate Your Card at HomeDepot.com/MyCard

Visit HomeDepot.com/MyCard and follow the secure steps. You’ll need your card number and personal details to verify your identity. Once activated, register for online access to manage your account anytime.

2. Shop In-Store and Online

Use your Home Depot credit card at checkout—swipe, insert, or tap in-store, or enter details online at HomeDepot.com. You’ll also unlock exclusive financing offers that update regularly.

3. Take Advantage of Special Financing

Enjoy flexible financing on large purchases with deferred interest or fixed monthly payment options. Check your account for current promotions before shopping.

4. Manage Your Account Online

  • View balance and available credit
  • Make one-time or scheduled payments
  • Review past statements
  • Update personal info
  • Set alerts to avoid missed due dates

All your details in one place to keep spending organized.

5. Pay on Time to Maximize Benefits

Always pay at least the minimum balance on time. To avoid interest, pay your full balance. Use the secure portal at HomeDepot.com/MyCard with your bank account.

Benefits of Using a Home Depot Credit Card

  • Special Financing Offers – Pay over time without immediate interest (if paid in full within the promo period).
  • Easy Online Management – View balances, payments, and purchases in one place.
  • Exclusive Cardholder Deals – Enjoy discounts and member-only savings.
  • Budget Control – Separate your home improvement spending from other expenses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I activate my Home Depot credit card?
A: Visit HomeDepot.com/MyCard, select “Activate,” and follow the steps.

Q: Can I use my card both online and in-store?
A: Yes, it works at all Home Depot stores and on HomeDepot.com.

Q: How do I make payments?
A: Log into HomeDepot.com/MyCard to pay from your bank account or set up AutoPay.

Q: What happens if I miss a payment?
A: Late fees and interest may apply, and your credit score can be impacted. Always pay on time.
